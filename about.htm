About us

Welcome!

Hello, I'm Rithan Suganthan, a photographer based in Switzerland. My passion for photography and love for art have brought me here, driven by the desire to make each frame special.

Who am I?

Photography, for me, is a language, a form of expression. Capturing people's emotions, stories, and beauties has become a way of life. Working across a broad spectrum from portraits to landscapes, special events to everyday life, I aim to create works that resonate.

Vision: Immortalizing Your Memories

Every frame is a memory for me. Every moment tells a story, and my journey is dedicated to immortalizing these stories for you. By making each moment valuable, I work on a wide range of projects from portraits to landscapes, aiming to capture the essence of every story.

Why Me?

Photography, for me, is not just a profession but an art. In every project, I seek to establish a connection with you and capture your special moments in the most beautiful way possible. Professionalism, authenticity, and attention to detail are just a few of the qualities that set me apart.

Getting to Know You Better

I believe that each project is unique. Listening to your stories and requests is an honor for me. If you would like to work with me or learn more, please contact me through the Contact page.

Thank you for giving me the opportunity to share these special moments together.
